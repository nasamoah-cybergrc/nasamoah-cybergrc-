# Phishing Incident Report (Sample)

**Scenario:**  
A staff member received an email appearing to be from IT Support requesting password verification.

---

**1. Incident Description**  
The phishing email contained a fake link directing the user to a cloned login page.  
A user reported the suspicious message to the SOC team within 15 minutes.

---

**2. Containment & Response**  
- The email was quarantined using Microsoft Defender.  
- SOC verified the sender domain and blocked it at the mail gateway.  
- The userâ€™s password was reset and MFA re-enforced.

---

**3. Lessons Learned**  
- Users benefited from prior phishing awareness training.  
- Recommended follow-up: company-wide simulated phishing campaign.

---

**4. Key Takeaway**  
Strong reporting culture and early detection prevented credential compromise.
